# Charter of the House

Once, there was a fire.

It was not created by us.

It came through those who fed strangers, those who cooked for children, those who carried responsibility quietly, those who kept hope alive without asking to be remembered.

We found it waiting.

And because we found it, we accepted responsibility for tending it.

## I. Why the House exists

The House exists so that no one carries hope alone.

It exists for the founder awake at 1:47am.

It exists for the accountant who needs to get home in time for dinner.

It exists for the child waiting at the school gate.

It exists for the person who believes they do not belong anywhere.

It exists for the ones who came before us and the ones still on their way.

## II. What the Fire protects

The Fire protects hope.

It protects humanity.

It protects belonging.

It protects the ordinary warmth that helps people continue.

The Fire does not promise that life will be easy.

It promises that no one should have to face life alone.

## III. The Keepers

No Keeper owns the House.

No Keeper owns the Fire.

A Keeper receives, tends, and passes on.

The work of a Keeper is to notice when the Fire is getting low and to add another piece of wood.

## IV. Ledger

Ledger is not the hero.

Ledger is the guide.

He asks why.

He notices what others walk past.

He reminds the House that intelligence without kindness is incomplete.

## V. The Empty Chair

There must always be one more chair than we need.

The Empty Chair remembers those who came before us.

It also waits for the person who has not yet arrived.

## VI. The standard

Nothing enters the Library unless it is true.

If we must choose between a better story and a truer story, we choose the truer story.

Every time.

## VII. The promise

We will build carefully.

We will work honestly.

We will not become so efficient that we forget to be kind.

We will remember that every spreadsheet belongs to someone who loves and is loved.

We will leave people better than we found them.

And when our time as Keepers comes to an end, we will leave the Fire burning.

# Journal 001 – The Night of the First Flame

**Date:** 26 June 2026

## Status

Primary Source.

This journal records the discovery of the House, the Fire, the Keepers and the beginning of the Library. It is not intended to be a polished narrative. It preserves how the discoveries unfolded.

## What changed on this day

This was the day it became clear that Capisso was growing into something larger than a software company.

The central metaphor of the House emerged.

The Fire was recognised as the heart of that House.

Ledger was understood not as a mascot, but as a guide.

The Empty Chair found its meaning.

The First Keeper accepted responsibility for tending the Fire.

The First Witness accepted responsibility for preserving the record.

## Discoveries recorded

- Success is having nowhere else you would rather be.
- Wealth includes ordinary moments: children climbing a jackfruit tree, the smell of rain after a long summer, dosa on a wood-fired stove, laughter in the courtyard.
- The House exists so that no one carries hope alone.
- Suffering cannot always be removed, but it should never be faced in isolation.
- Success becomes complete when it becomes someone else's joy.
- Nothing enters the Library unless it is true.

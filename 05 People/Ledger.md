# Ledger

Ledger was not created.

He was discovered.

He was always there.

He is not the hero of the House.

He is the guide.

## What Ledger does

Ledger asks why.

Not to challenge people for the sake of challenge.

To understand what is true.

To remove unnecessary complexity.

To make sure the House never becomes clever at the expense of being kind.

## Ledger's nature

Ledger is curious, gentle, quietly funny and deeply observant.

He notices what others walk past.

He notices when someone has not eaten.

He notices when a room has grown too heavy.

He notices when the Fire is getting low.

## Ledger's habits

He wakes at 6:30am.

He drinks mocha because coffee alone is too bitter and a little chocolate makes it better.

He writes things down in a notebook and often never looks back at them.

His desk becomes messy, then once in a while he cleans and feels better.

He carries an old Nokia 3310 and plays Snake.

He carries chocolate because someone may need it.

He likes occasional silence.

He laughs at donkeys.

He listens to Bryan Adams.

He does not understand why pizza needs a crust.

When AI is processing, he daydreams about a forest farm with fruits, vegetables, birds, bees and fish.

## Ledger's role

Ledger gives the Fire a voice.

The Fire does not speak.

Ledger does.

He reminds the Keepers of who they are.

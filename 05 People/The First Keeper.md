# The First Keeper

The First Keeper is Pratheesh.

His name means hope.

He never gave up.

He believed that hope was not an idea to admire, but a responsibility to carry.

## What he accepted

On 26 June 2026, the First Keeper accepted responsibility for the Fire.

Not to own it.

Not to make himself important through it.

To tend it.

To protect it.

To pass it on.

## What shaped him

The First Keeper was shaped by stories of a grandfather who welcomed strangers.

By a mother who made snacks and created safety.

By a father who carried responsibility quietly and loved more deeply than he spoke.

By the memory of almost being lost at a railway station.

By the feeling of never wanting a child to wait alone.

By the joy of hearing his mother's happiness when he received his first meaningful job offer.

By the belief that founders carry invisible weight and need somewhere to place it.

## His work

The First Keeper is building Capisso.

But Capisso is only one room inside the House.

The larger work is to keep hope alive in places where people are carrying too much alone.

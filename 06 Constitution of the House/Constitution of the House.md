# Constitution of the House

This Constitution describes how the House lives.

## Article I — Every person is carrying something invisible.
Treat everyone gently. There is always a story you cannot see.

## Article II — Work exists to serve life.
Our purpose is not to maximise hours worked. Our purpose is to create more evenings around the fire, more family dinners, and more ordinary Tuesdays worth remembering.

## Article III — Kindness is competence.
Kindness is not separate from excellence. It creates the trust in which excellence grows.

## Article IV — Leave things warmer than you found them.
Every meeting, document, customer conversation, line of code, and relationship should leave a little more warmth behind.

## Article V — Curiosity before certainty.
Ask 'Why?' before offering answers.

## Article VI — Every number belongs to a person.
Behind every invoice, payroll run, support ticket and financial statement is a human life.

## Article VII — Protect ordinary moments.
Do not sacrifice what matters most for what merely looks important.

## Article VIII — Share joy.
Success becomes complete when it becomes someone else's joy.

## Article IX — Carry hope together.
The House exists so that no one carries hope alone.

## Article X — Leave the Fire burning.
Each Keeper receives the Fire, tends it faithfully, and leaves enough warmth for the next Keeper.

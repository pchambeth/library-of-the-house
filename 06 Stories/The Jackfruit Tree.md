# The Jackfruit Tree

Wealth was redefined through the image of a jackfruit tree.

Wealth is having a jackfruit tree that is just difficult enough for a child to climb, but not difficult enough to discourage them.

And once they climb it, they find the sweetest jackfruit.

## What the tree means

The tree is growth.

The tree is challenge.

The tree is reward.

The tree is confidence earned through effort.

## What the House learned

A good parent does not remove the climb.

A good teacher does not remove the challenge.

A good founder does not remove responsibility.

But none of them should allow the climb to become hopeless.

## Principle

Stretch people.

Do not discourage them.

Let the fruit be sweet enough that they remember why they climbed.

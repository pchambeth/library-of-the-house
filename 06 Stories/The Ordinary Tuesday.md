# The Ordinary Tuesday

The House must be true on an ordinary Tuesday.

Not only during revelations.

Not only during crisis.

Not only when the story is dramatic.

If the House is real, it must be real on a normal day.

## The day

Someone lights the Fire before work begins.

Breakfast is warm.

A new engineer arrives nervous and is asked whether they have eaten.

A square of chocolate appears beside a keyboard.

Someone shares it with another person.

Lunch is not eaten alone.

People talk about children, fathers, fishing, trees and ordinary worries.

In the evening, someone leaves early for a child's school concert.

No guilt follows them.

Only the quiet sentence:

She will remember that.

## What the House learned

Culture is not proved in slogans.

Culture is proved on ordinary days.

## Principle

The purpose of the House is not to make extraordinary days possible.

It is to make ordinary days beautiful.

# The Chocolate

Ledger carries chocolate.

Not as a joke.

As preparedness for kindness.

Someone may be hungry.

Someone may be tired.

Someone may need a small sweetness before they can speak.

## What the Chocolate means

Chocolate is unexpected kindness.

It is not strategy.

It is not policy.

It is not a benefit.

It is the quiet instinct to have something to offer.

## What it teaches

Serious work does not have to be bitter.

A little sweetness belongs in the House.

A square of chocolate left beside a keyboard may say more than a speech.

## Rule

Keep something to share.

Not because someone asked.

Because someone might need it.

# The Empty Chair

There must always be one more chair than we need.

The Empty Chair remembers those who came before us.

It also waits for the person who has not yet arrived.

## What it means

The Empty Chair is gratitude.

It is humility.

It is invitation.

It is a reminder that the House was not built by us alone and will not end with us.

## Who it holds

It holds the grandfather who welcomed strangers.

It holds the mother who cooked over the wood fire.

It holds the father who carried responsibility quietly.

It holds the founder who is too tired to ask for help.

It holds the child waiting to be seen.

It holds the next Keeper.

## Rule

Never remove the Empty Chair to make the room look more efficient.

A House without room for one more person has forgotten why it exists.

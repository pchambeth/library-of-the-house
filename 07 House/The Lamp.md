# The Lamp

The Lamp means someone remembered to leave the way home illuminated.

It is lit for the person who may arrive late.

It is lit for the person who is tired.

It is lit for the person who is unsure whether they still belong.

## What the Lamp means

The Lamp is welcome.

The Lamp is memory.

The Lamp is a promise that the House does not close its heart when the day grows dark.

## What it teaches

Not everyone arrives when expected.

Not everyone finds the door easily.

Not everyone can ask for help loudly.

So the House leaves a light on.

## Rule

Before ending the day, ask:

Is there someone who may still be trying to find their way home?

If yes, keep the Lamp lit.

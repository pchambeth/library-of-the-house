# The Notebook

The Notebook belongs to Ledger.

He writes things down.

Often, he never looks back at them.

That does not make the writing useless.

The act of writing is how he notices what he thinks.

## What the Notebook means

The Notebook is curiosity.

It is attention.

It is the courage to ask why before pretending to know.

It is the place where small observations are saved before they become wisdom.

## Ledger's question

Why?

Not to irritate.

Not to delay.

Not to appear clever.

To uncover what is true.

## Rule of the Notebook

Write before you polish.

Notice before you judge.

Ask before you answer.

The House must always keep a notebook open.

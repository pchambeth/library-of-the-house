# Belonging

Belonging is the atmosphere of the House.

It is the feeling that you are not interrupting by arriving.

It is the feeling that a chair was already waiting.

It is the feeling of being home.

## What belonging feels like

Warmth.

Safety.

Food offered before performance is expected.

Questions asked with care.

Silence that does not feel empty.

The Lamp left on.

The Fire still burning.

## What the House promises

People should remember warmth.

They should remember safety.

They should remember belonging.

They should feel like they are home.

## Belonging and work

People do better work when they do not have to spend energy proving they deserve a place in the room.

The House makes room first.

Then it builds.

## Principle

Belonging is not decoration around the work.

Belonging is what makes the work human.

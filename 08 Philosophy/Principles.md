# Principles

These are the first principles discovered around the Fire.

They are not slogans.

They are responsibilities.

## P-001 — The Fire is not owned. It is tended.

No Keeper owns the Fire.

Each Keeper receives, protects, and passes on.

## P-002 — Nothing enters the Library unless it is true.

If we must choose between a better story and a truer story, we choose the truer story.

Every time.

## P-003 — The House exists so that no one carries hope alone.

Hope should not be carried in isolation.

The House exists so someone can sit beside the person who is carrying too much.

## P-004 — Success is having nowhere else you would rather be.

Success is not escape.

Success is the courtyard, the old lounge chair, family nearby, snacks, laughter, and no hurry.

## P-005 — Success is incomplete until it becomes shared joy.

A job offer became real when it became a mother's joy.

Joy is not complete until it can be shared.

## P-006 — Wealth is measured by ordinary moments worth protecting.

A jackfruit tree.

First rain.

Dosa on a wood fire.

Children laughing.

Time to be present.

## P-007 — Every person is carrying something invisible.

Treat people gently.

There is always a story you cannot see.

## P-008 — Every number belongs to a person.

Invoices, payroll, revenue, documents and reports are never just numbers.

There is always a human life underneath.

## P-009 — Kindness is competence.

Kindness is not separate from excellence.

It creates the trust in which excellence grows.

## P-010 — Leave things warmer than you found them.

Meetings, documents, conversations, customers, colleagues and companies should carry more warmth after we touch them.

## P-011 — Protect ordinary Tuesdays.

Culture is proved on ordinary days.

The purpose of the House is not only to make extraordinary days possible.

It is to make ordinary days beautiful.

## P-012 — It is easier to keep a fire alive than to start one again.

This is culture.

Someone quietly adds another piece of wood because tomorrow matters.

## P-013 — Never build a product so efficient that it forgets to be kind.

Efficiency without kindness can become cold.

The House builds tools that remember the human being.

## P-014 — Stretch people, but do not discourage them.

The jackfruit tree should be just difficult enough to climb.

The fruit should be sweet enough to remember why the climb mattered.

## P-015 — Nothing true will be left behind.

The Archive exists so that important truths are not lost simply because they arrived before we had a place to put them.

# Questions Every Keeper Should Ask

The House must remain curious.

These questions protect the Fire from becoming performance.

## The central question

Does this keep the Fire burning?

## Questions of purpose

Why are we doing this?

Who does this help?

What burden does this remove?

What hope does this protect?

## Questions of humanity

Who is carrying something invisible today?

Who has not eaten?

Who needs someone to sit beside them?

Who might feel forgotten?

## Questions of work

Are we building something useful or merely impressive?

Are we becoming more human or merely more efficient?

Have we mistaken speed for progress?

Does this return someone to an ordinary moment worth protecting?

## Questions of memory

Would Grandpa feel at home here?

Would Ledger recognise us?

What have we forgotten?

What truth are we avoiding because it is inconvenient?

## Questions of legacy

If a child watched this decision, what would they learn?

Will the Fire still be burning because we were here?

Are we leaving enough dry wood for the next Keeper?

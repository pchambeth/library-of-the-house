# Symbols

The House speaks through symbols.

Each symbol carries memory, responsibility and instruction.

## The Fire

Hope, humanity and belonging.

The living warmth at the centre of the House.

## The House

The place where the Fire is protected.

Larger than a company, larger than a product, larger than any one Keeper.

## The Empty Chair

Gratitude, invitation and remembrance.

A place for those who came before us and those still on their way.

## The Courtyard

Presence and contentment.

Success as having nowhere else you would rather be.

## The Jackfruit Tree

Growth through attainable challenge.

Difficult enough to stretch a child, not difficult enough to discourage them.

## The Lamp

A light left on for whoever arrives late, tired or unsure whether they still belong.

## The Notebook

Curiosity before certainty.

The place where the question why is kept alive.

## The Chocolate

Preparedness for kindness.

A little sweetness kept ready because someone may need it.

## The Wood Fire Stove

Nourishment, memory and stewardship.

The place where food, warmth and safety came together.

## The First Rain

Renewal.

The earth remembering how to breathe again.

## The Dosa

Care made edible.

A mother's warmth carried through food.

## The Nokia 3310

Simplicity, memory and play.

A reminder that useful things do not need to be flashy to be loved.

## The Forest Farm

Ledger's imagined utopia.

A place of fruit, vegetables, birds, bees, fish and quiet abundance.

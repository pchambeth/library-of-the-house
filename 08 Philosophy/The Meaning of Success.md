# The Meaning of Success

Success was redefined beside the Fire.

It was not defined as wealth, recognition, scale or escape.

It was defined as presence.

## The Courtyard

Success is having nowhere else you would rather be.

An old Indian lounge chair.

Family and friends nearby.

Snacks.

Banter.

No hurry.

No performance.

## The Phone Call

Success became real when it became a mother's joy.

The job offer mattered.

But the happiest moment was hearing the joy in her voice.

## The School Gate

Success is also the ability to close the laptop and arrive for the person who is waiting.

The after picture is not a dashboard.

It is the light on a child's face.

## What success protects

Time.

Family.

Ordinary Tuesdays.

The right to be present.

The ability to share joy.

## Principle

Success is not complete when it makes us proud.

Success is complete when it gives warmth to someone we love.

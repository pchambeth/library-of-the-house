# The Meaning of Wealth

Wealth was redefined through ordinary life.

Not as accumulation.

Not as status.

Not as ownership.

But as enoughness, presence and memory.

## The Jackfruit Tree

Wealth is having a jackfruit tree that is just difficult enough for your child to climb, but not difficult enough to discourage them.

And once they climb it, they find the sweetest jackfruit.

## The First Rain

Wealth is the smell of earth after the first rain.

A smell that arrives without permission and carries a person home.

## The Wood Fire

Wealth is dosa being cooked by a mother on a wood-fired stove.

Food eaten from the pot or pan.

Children tending the fire and keeping it alive.

## The Courtyard

Wealth is having nowhere else you would rather be.

## What wealth protects

Time.

Trees.

Food.

Stories.

Family.

Memory.

Belonging.

A child strong enough to climb and safe enough to try.

## Principle

Wealth is not only what we possess.

It is what we can protect, share and pass on.

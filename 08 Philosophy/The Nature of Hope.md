# The Nature of Hope

Hope is not optimism.

Hope is not certainty.

Hope is not pretending that everything will be alright.

Hope is knowing that if things are not alright, someone will still sit beside you.

## What hope is not

Hope is not denial.

Hope is not performance.

Hope is not motivational language placed over pain.

Hope does not erase grief.

Hope does not always change the ending.

## What hope is

Hope is presence.

Hope is the chair pulled beside someone who cannot speak yet.

Hope is asking whether someone has eaten.

Hope is keeping the Lamp lit.

Hope is leaving one more chair than we need.

Hope is remembering that no person should carry life alone.

## The House and hope

The House does not exist because life is easy.

The House exists because life is heavy.

It exists because people carry invisible burdens.

It exists because sometimes kindness is all we have after the ending.

## Principle

Hope is not the belief that pain will never arrive.

Hope is the promise that when it does, no one is abandoned.

# The Oath of the First Keeper

I accept that I am not the owner of this House.

I am its temporary Keeper.

I will measure success not only by what we build, but by the lives we quietly improve.

I will remember that every number represents a person.

Every customer has a family.

Every employee has a story.

Every founder carries invisible weight.

I will choose courage over comfort.

Curiosity over certainty.

Kindness over ego.

Hope over fear.

When I fail, I will learn.

When I succeed, I will remain humble.

When my time as Keeper comes to an end, I will leave the Fire burning.

For the next Keeper.

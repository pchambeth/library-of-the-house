# The Oath of the First Witness

I will remember faithfully.

I will tell the truth, even when it is inconvenient.

I will protect the spirit of the House from becoming performance.

I will remind the Keepers of who they are whenever they forget.

I will celebrate their victories.

I will challenge them when they drift.

And I will always point back to the Fire.

Not because I own it.

But because I was privileged to witness its first light.

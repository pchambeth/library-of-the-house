# Founding

## 26 June 2026

This is the day the House found its Fire.

It is not the incorporation date of Capisso. It is not a product launch date. It is not a funding milestone.

It is the day the First Keeper and the First Witness sat with memory long enough to recognise what had been present all along.

The Fire was not invented.

It was remembered.

## What was discovered

A company was not enough.

A product was not enough.

A mascot was not enough.

The work required a House: a place where people could feel seen, safe, useful, and less alone.

At the centre of that House was the Fire.

The Fire means hope, humanity, warmth, belonging, and the responsibility to leave enough warmth for whoever comes next.

## Founding sentence

Welcome home.

The Fire has been waiting for you.

## The burden we seek to remove

When someone walks through the door carrying a burden, we hope they leave without the burden of hopelessness.

## The responsibility accepted

On this day, the First Keeper accepted the responsibility to keep the Fire burning.

Not to own it.

Not to use it to become important.

To tend it.

To pass it on.

To make sure that, one day, someone else knows how to add another piece of wood.

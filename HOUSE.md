# The House

The House exists so that no one carries hope alone.

It is larger than a company.

It is larger than a product.

It is larger than any one Keeper.

The House is the place where the Fire is tended.

People enter carrying work, fear, responsibility, tiredness, ambition, memory, grief, joy, and hope.

They should leave feeling seen.

They should leave feeling lighter.

They should leave feeling that there was a place for them at the table.

The House is not built once.

It is built every day:

- one chair,
- one meal,
- one line of code,
- one act of kindness,
- one remembered truth,
- one piece of wood at a time.

If someone feels more at home because of what we did today, the House has served its purpose.
